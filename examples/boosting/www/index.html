Understanding Page Boosting

The hx-boost attribute intercepts standard anchor clicks and form submissions, converting them into AJAX requests. It then swaps the page body and merges head elements without a full page reload, resulting in smooth transitions.

Standard Navigation

Causes a full browser refresh, clearing page state and flashing the browser window.

No Boost

Boosted Navigation

Performs an AJAX swap of the body, merging stylesheets and executing new scripts smoothly.

hx-boost="true"
